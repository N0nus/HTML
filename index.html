How You Can Build Your Dream PC

Introduction to Building a PC

Building your own PC can be a fun and rewarding experience. You get to both design and assemble a computer tailored exactly to your needs. Whether you're looking to create a powerful gaming rig, a high-performance workstation, or just a reliable everyday computer, building it yourself allows you to choose the best components for your budget and purpose.

Because the first time building one can be a daunting task, I've created this simple guide as a way to collect all the information you need to build a PC in one place. I'll give tips on what kind of a PC to build, which parts to select, and how to assemble it. I have also included a list of the best resources for building a PC so you can have all the information needed to make this a fun and easy experience. Click next to continue the guide.
